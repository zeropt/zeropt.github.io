Photo Gallery

In 2023 I bought a camera to take with me on hikes and other adventures. Though I shoot for fun and don't post to any social media platforms, I thought it might be nice to make a small gallery here on my personal website.

Here is the gear I currently use:

  • Camera: Panasonic Lumix GX85
  • Wide Lens: Rokinon 12mm f/2 NCS CS
  • Standard Lens: Lumix G 25mm f/1.7 ASPH
  • Zoom Lens: Lumix G Vario 35-100mm f/4-5.6 ASPH Mega O.I.S.
  • Reflex Lens: Tokina Reflex 300mm f/6.3 MF Macro
